FAVORITE MOVIES LIST

TOP 3 BOLLYWOOD MOVIES OF ALL TIME

1. Sholay (1975)

A timeless action-packed classic with unforgettable characters and dialogue, offering a mix of drama, humor, and emotion.

2. Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)

A quintessential romance that redefined Bollywood love stories, with beautiful chemistry between the leads and iconic songs.

3. Gully Boy (2019)

A gritty and inspiring journey of a street rapper from Mumbai, showcasing powerful performances and a fresh perspective on Indian music and youth culture.
